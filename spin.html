Spin Quantum Number (s)

What is "s"?

The **spin quantum number (s)** determines the **spin direction** of an electron.

🔹 Electron Spin States:

  • Spin up: `+½` (⬆️)
  • Spin down: `−½` (⬇️)

🎨 Fun Fact:

Quantum spin is used in **MRI scans** to detect brain activity!
