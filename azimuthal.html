Azimuthal Quantum Number (ℓ)

What is "ℓ"?

The **azimuthal quantum number (ℓ)** determines the shape of an electron’s orbital.

🔹 Orbital Types:

  • s-orbital (ℓ=0): **Spherical** 🌐
  • p-orbital (ℓ=1): **Dumbbell** 🥜
  • d-orbital (ℓ=2): **Double dumbbell** ✨
  • f-orbital (ℓ=3): **Complex multi-lobed** 🤯
